2028 OLYMPIC GAMES PLAN

The Olympic Games are the world’s leading international sporting event, highlighting ultimate athletic achievement and bringing together athletes from around the world to compete at the highest level across a wide array of sports. Originating in ancient Greece and revived in 1896, the modern Olympics are held every four years in different Host Cities across the globe. The Olympics promote friendship, respect and excellence, captivating and uniting the world in the spirit of peaceful competition.

We are so proud to carry LA’s legacy as an Olympic Host City forward. July 14, 2028 will mark Los Angeles’ third time hosting the Olympics, following the 1932 and 1984 Games.
